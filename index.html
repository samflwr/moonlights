work in progress.

my name is samir buzatu (xvmir) and i’m a digital crafter. wherever and with whomever i’m working, i let the needs of a project dictate its style. i try not to think my way into a design, quality is always my priority but i believe you have to let play drive you.

i am a continuous learner. learning for me equates to exploring new ideas and growing as an individual. i just cannot imagine my life devoid of designing. i freely express my creativity and imagination by designing.

easy is boring. if you aren’t having fun pushing yourself, you aren’t doing it right.
